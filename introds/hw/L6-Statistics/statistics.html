Is the correlation reliable?

How come if the p-value is very small, indicating that we can discard a null-hypotesis, but our pearson correlation coefficient indicated a weak correlation?

The answer is data size, our data size is just way to big, which will almost alwasy will make the p-value very small. With big data everything will be significant. All variations will be just to small

Thus in short, it is not reliable
